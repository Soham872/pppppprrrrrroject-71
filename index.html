Campus Life

Sports and performing arts centre/SPA building

Was established in 2008

The SPA centre is next to the senior school building and is used for sport, ACS and performing arts. It also holds assembly and chapel.

The 6/7 Building

Was established in 2021

The 6/7 Building houses the year 6 and 7 classrooms.

The Main Library

Was established in 2000

The Main library is in the middle school and has all different books.

school achievements

ACS year 7 Tennis vs Loyola
Won against Loyola in ACS tennis

On the last week of the term the year 7 tennis team beat the loyola year 7 tennis team in acs. OACC won 69-42 on sets.

ACS Chess final
The ACS Chess team came first place at the finals

On the last day of the term the ACS Chess team played the finals against westbourne. They lost 2 matches and won 12 matches winning the trophy.

ACS debate on Back Burning
Won an ACS Debate on Back Burning

The Debate team won the ACS debate on Back burning against 4 other teams from Westbourne, St. Micheal's, Loyola and St Leonard's.

Whats New

  • Online Learning

    As the city goes into lockdown, we are going to have online learning. The students will meet with their teachers at the start of every period then go off and do their work. We will have this for a month before getting onto campus.

  • New canteen in the 6/7 area

    The 6/7 building has got a brand new canteen with a much diverse menu of food. Wraps, Burgers, Chips, Hotdogs, you name it this new canteen has it.
